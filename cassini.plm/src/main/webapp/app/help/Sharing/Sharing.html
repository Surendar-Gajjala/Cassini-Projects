What is the use of sharing?

            Manufacturer can share an item with groups, internal and external users, all shared items will be displayed here, sharing item also has access permissions and user can securely share items with all types of users.

 

How to share an Item with Internal or External user?

1. Select items from navigation bar

2. Click on an item

3. Click on Share icon

4. Share Item panel opens

5. Select share type (Person or Group)

6. Select User/Group

7. Select Permissions (Read/Write)

8. Click on Share (item shared successfully)

How to view Shared items view?

1. Select Sharing from navigation bar

2. Click on item number

3. It will be directed to item details view (All details related to item will be displayed based on access permissions).
