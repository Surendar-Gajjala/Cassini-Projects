Cassini.PLM is a fully configurable Product Lifecycle Management solution from Cassini Systems for all design and manufacturing organizations. Cassini PLM is an end to end solution for managing the Product Data, BOM Management, Change Management and AML (Approved Manufacturing List). Cassini PLM is designed to configure the data model for customer specific needs and business process enabling easy configuration and reduce the customization efforts thus making it faster to deploy and run productively.
