What is Share Item?

            Users can share one or more items with other users with permissions, this will allow manufacturing organisations to share items safely with external or internal users.

Why do we need to Share item?

            Item sharing is an additional feature on item, it is useful in sharing items securely with edit permissions.

How to Share item?

 

1. Slect Item/Items

2. Click on "Share Items" button,popup will appear

 

3. Select Person/Group

4. Select User/Group

5. Select Permission

6. Click on Share
