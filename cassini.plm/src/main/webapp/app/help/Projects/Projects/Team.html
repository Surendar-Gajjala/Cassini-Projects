What is Team and why do we need to create a Team?

            Team is nothing but a group of employees who are working on a project. It is mandatory to add team members to the project, without adding team members we cannot assign tasks.

 

How to add Team members to project?

 

1.    Go to Team tab

2.    Click on "+" symbol to Add Member

3.    Select the Person(s) panel opens

4.  Select person(s) and

5.    Click on Add button

Selected Persons will be added to the Project Team successfully
