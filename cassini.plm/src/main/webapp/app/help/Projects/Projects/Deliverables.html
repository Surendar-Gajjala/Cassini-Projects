What is Deliverables and why do we need to Create Deliverables?

            Deliverables are nothing but a end product which is delivered to customer. Adding deliverables to Project/Activity/Task will help all team members to get an idea about what they need to work to deliver finished product.

 

How to add Deliverables to Project?

 

1.    Go to Deliverables tab

2.    Click on Add Deliverables button

3.    Add Deliverables panel opens

4.  Select Items and

5.    Click on Add button

Selected Deliverables will be added successfully

 

How to Delete Deliverables?

1. Go to Deliverables tab

2. Select Delete Deliverable from actions button

3. Confirmation popup will appear "Click on OK"

Selected deliverable will be deleted.
