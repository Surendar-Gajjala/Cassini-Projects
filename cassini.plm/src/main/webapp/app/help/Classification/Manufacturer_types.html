What is MANUFACTURERE type?

            Manufacturer type is another pre-defined classification type, it will be useful to classify manufacturers by their segment of business (Hardware, Software)

 

Why do we need to CREATE Manufacturer type?

There may be 100’s of manufacturers supplying different types of materials into the organisation, it is better and useful to categorize them based on business type.

 

How to Create/View/Delete Manufacturer type?

CREATE MANUFACTURER TYPES

 

1.         Click on Right mouse button on Manufacturer part (Click Add Type)

2.    Fill in Manufacturer Type Name

 

DELETE MANUFACTURER TYPE

 

1. Click Right mouse button, then Delete Type

2. Confirmation dialogue will open, click on OK
