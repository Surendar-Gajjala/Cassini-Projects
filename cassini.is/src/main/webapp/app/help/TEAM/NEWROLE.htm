STEPS TO CREATE NEW ROLE

 

  1. click on “Team”.

  2. Click on "Role"

  3. click on “New Role” button, then popup will appear.

  4.  Enter “Name”, “Description”.

  5. click on “Create”, will get “Role created successfully” message.

  6.  To delete existed role click on “Delete” button, then it will show” Role deleted successfully” message

  7.  same steps should follow for adding number of roles.
