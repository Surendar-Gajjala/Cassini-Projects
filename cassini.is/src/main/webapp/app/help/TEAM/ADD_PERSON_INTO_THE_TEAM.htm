ADD A PERSON INTO THE TEAM

 

A. Click on Navigation icon.

B. Click on "Dashboard".

 

C. Select "Project".

 

 

  1. Click on “TEAM” module.

  2. Click on "Team".

  3. Click on “Add Person” button, it’s taken to the new screen.

  4. Select the employee names whom you are going to add in to the Team.

  5. Click on “Add” button, will get  “Team created successfully” message.

  6. "X" will help u close the current window and it's taken to the previous window.
