LOAN ISSUED

 

1.Click on "Loan Issued functionality".

2.Click on "Add Loan",popup will appear.

3.Click on "Auto",number will generate.

4.Select "From Project".

5.Select "To Project".

6.Select "To Store".

7.Click on "Add" ,will get success message.

 

 

8.Click on Load Number,Status should be "PENDING" until return all items from store.

9.Click on "Items".

10.Click on "Add Items",Popup will appear.

11.Enter "Loan Quantity".

12.Click on "Add".

13.Check the Loan Issued items in "Loan Received" under Issued Store (To Store).
