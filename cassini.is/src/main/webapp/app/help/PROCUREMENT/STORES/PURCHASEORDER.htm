PURCHASE ORDER

 

1.Click on "Purchase order" functionality.

2.Click on "New Purchase Order",its taken to the new window.

 

 

3.Select Supplier.

4.Enter "PO Date"(Mandatory).

5.Enter Note (optional).

6.Click on "Add Items".

7.Select "Request".

8.Click Add(+) button.

9.Click on cancel (X) to close the window.

 


 

10.To remove Item Click on "Remove button(-)".

11.Click on Create will get sucess message.

 

 

APPROVE INDENT

 

12.Click on Purchase Order Number,Its taken to the detail view.

13.Intial status of PO is "New".

 

 

 

14.To edit "Notes","Purchase Qty" Click on "Edit" button.

15.To delete Item Click on "Delete" button .

16.Enter "Approved By".

17.Click on Approve,Will get success message.

 

 

18.Staus Will Change from "New" to "Approved".
