BOQ

 

BOQ creation is as simple as that you are working with an excel sheet, you can add any number items with Item Number, Name, Units, Unit Price and Quantity complete information. You can get the Item Total in the same sheet all information at one place and you can create multiple sheets.

 

STEPS TO CREATE BOQ

 

1. Go to "BOQ" Module.okoololo9lol

2.Click on "+(New BOQ Group) button".

3.Enter Group name.

4.  Click on “”, It will pop up a window.

 

5.  Click on "Select Type".

6.  Click on “Type".

 

 

7..   Select All/Single Item.

 

 

8.Click on "Select"

9.Enter "Quantity".

10.Click on "Save",will get success message.
